Who we are

Our Resident's Guide is intended to give those who are contemplating the move to Hillbrow a brief overview of who we are, what we do and the services available. It forms part of your contract should you decide to join us

QUALITY IN CARE

We encourage friends and family to make use of the tea and coffee making facilties when the kitchen is not being used for meal preparation. In this way you, the resident, can in- vite visitors to have a cup of tea or coffee whenever you like.

Why us
WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM US

We ensure that all residents are treated as individuals within the home and that your rights are protected. We encourage and enable residents to lead a life that is fulfilled.

In-Home Care

Staff are always on duty to provide the necessary care and support. Each Resident is registered with the local General Practitioner and is entitled to request a home visit just as if they were living at home. Community nurses also carry out any dressings, blood tests etc... as required.

Nursing Support

If you are able to look after your own medication, then you can continue to do so. For those who do not wish to self medicate then the Staff are trained to do so in accordance with the Pharmaceutical Society of Gt. Britain guidelines.

Rehabilitation

We provide Entertainments like Musical Events, Quizzes, Social Activities, Books & Newspapers, Resident forums and much more. These are organised by our Activities Coordinator who organises activties on a regular basis.

Memory Care

We provide a safe, nurturing environment tailored to support residents, ensuring their dignity and quality of life. With compassionate staff and personalized care plans, we help residents preserve cherished memories while creating meaningful moments every day.

About Us

The Hillbrow Residential Care Home has an enviable reputation for providing attentive, considerate, safe and re- spectful care within a very comfortable and well-furnished environment. Hillbrow was first registered as a Care Home with the Devon County Council in 1981 and is one of the longest established Care Homes in Crediton.
