About Us

The Hillbrow Residential Care Home has an enviable reputation for providing attentive, considerate, safe and re- spectful care within a very comfortable and well-furnished environment. Hillbrow was first registered as a Care Home with the Devon County Council in 1981 and is one of the longest established Care Homes in Crediton.

Hillbrow provides long term care services for 24 people aged over 65 years for both lady and gentlemen Residents

core values

By ensuring that our team of Carers, Housekeepers and Kitchen staff are trained and well led by the management of the Home you can have the confidence of knowing that you will be living in an environment that is caring, responsive to your assessed needs and effective by equipping you to find fulfilment witin the safe environment that is Hillbrow

Staff assisting with or providing personal care will always ensure privacy and respect for the resi- dent and will ensure that any relevant doors are not left open.

WHAT YOU CAN EXPECT FROM US

  • We ensure that all residents are treated as individuals within the home and that your rights are protected
  • We encourage and enable residents to lead a life that is fulfilled
  • We encourage and help to sustain the independence of our residents
  • We ensure that our residents are treated with respect and to conserve their dignity
  • We promote and observe the right of our residents to expect and receive their desired level of privacy
  • We promote, encourage and facilitate the right of residents to make choices that affect their everyday lives
  • We always safeguard our residents and ensure they are protected from abuse of any nature

HOW DO WE DO THIS?

  • Facilities within and around the Home
  • Provision is made for conversations with the Care Manager to be held in private if required
  • Residents are given the opportunity to make comments and express their wishes through regular resident's meetings or individual discussion
  • Residents will always be encouraged to choose what clothes to wear on a day-to-day basis, if they so wish
  • Residents may, if they wish to, incorporate personal items of furniture and ornaments withintheir bedrooms subject to Health and Safety regulations.
  • Personal choice of GP, Optician, Dentist and Chiropodist etc. is respected
  • Varied menus are provided and that residents preferences will be catered for where practicable
